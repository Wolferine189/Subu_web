Journey

My initial life

I was born in 2004 A.D. in a remote area of Dhading, Nepal, as the third child in my family, following my older brother and sister. While my earliest memories are somewhat hazy, a significant turning point in my life occurred when my father decided to relocate our entire family to Kathmandu. There, I embarked on my academic journey, commencing my education at the esteemed NAMESS (National Model English Secondary School) situated in Nepaltar, Kathmandu. My educational pursuit at NAMESS continued until the completion of my 7th grade. Subsequently, I furthered my studies at Tarun Mavi, culminating in the successful completion of my Secondary Education Examination (SEE).
To chase my aspirations and heed my calling, I made the deliberate choice to immerse myself in the Science Stream with a specialized focus on biology. This significant educational decision led me to NIST College (National Institute of Science and Technology), conveniently located in Lainchour, Kathmandu. My unwavering commitment and dedication to my academic journey were rewarded as I successfully completed my 12th standard with distinction. Currently, I am ardently pursuing my lifelong dream of becoming a Nurse, aiming to contribute meaningfully to the field of healthcare and make a positive impact on the lives of individuals in Nepal and beyond. This journey reflects my unyielding determination to transform dreams into reality and underscores my steadfast commitment to the pursuit of knowledge and personal growth.
